# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Clinic Administrators:** Manage schedules, coordinate queues, and handle billing/POS operations.
- **Doctors & Medical Staff:** View/edit patients' electronic medical records (EMR) and note clinical findings.
- **Nurses & Assistants:** Prep patients, update basic vitals, and coordinate physical queues.
- **Sales & Consultants:** Pitch packages, handle billing, and check inventory logs.
- **Clinic Owners:** Review high-level executive dashboards, financial reports, and system settings.

## Product Purpose
Anping ClinicHub is a comprehensive web application designed to run clinic operations in a single dashboard interface. Success is defined by highly efficient patient check-ins, real-time smart card OCR integration, smooth appointment calendar drag-and-drop actions, and error-free POS billing workflows.

## Brand Personality
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** Reassuring, secure, and precise.
- **Clean & Legible:** Clear typography utilizing the Kanit font for Thai legibility and JetBrains Mono for monospaced data.
- **Optimized & Efficient:** Responsive layout with minimal lag, optimized rendering for mobile, and intuitive spacing.

## Anti-references
- **Legacy Medical Software:** Cluttered screens with hundreds of tiny input fields, high cognitive load, and lack of visual flow.
- **Dull Grids & Excel Sheets:** Boring tables lacking contrast, proper margins, and interactive indicator badges.
- **Overly Playful Designs:** Large child-like buttons or highly saturated neon colors that diminish the professional feel of a medical portal.

## Design Principles
1. **Trust Through Precision:** Display medical information (HN, patient details, queue status) in distinct, high-contrast layouts.
2. **Action-oriented Denseness:** Balance white space and information density so clinic staff can view critical data at a glance without excessive scrolling.
3. **Smooth Adaptive Navigation:** Navigation bars and top headers should stay out of the way on mobile viewports while scrolling down, yet be easily retrievable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Typography Sizing:** Explicit minimum sizes for Thai text to ensure legibility on both desktop and mobile.
- **Varying Viewports:** High compatibility for tablet-sized viewports frequently used in clinical check-in stations.
- **Contrast Integrity:** Bold labels and dark ink-colored text over clean light backgrounds (slate-50/bg-white).
