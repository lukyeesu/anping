#### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Start here.** Does this look AI-generated?

**LLM assessment**: 
The app is a feature-rich clinic dashboard but displays clear "AI grammar" in its styling. There is an over-reliance on side-stripe accent borders (`border-l-4`) on cards and lists, which feels outdated and clutters the UI. Additionally, animating layout properties like `height` and `max-height` on scroll/hover leads to layout reflow stutter. The color mapping suffers from "SaaS-cliché" drift, using unconstrained grays and literal colors instead of strict design tokens.

**Deterministic scan**:
The automated detector (`detect.mjs`) flagged **230 design violations** in `src/App.jsx`:
- **175 gray-on-color text instances** (`gray-on-color`): Gray text on tinted backgrounds (e.g. `text-slate-700` or `text-slate-400` on `bg-sky-50`) which washes out reading contrast.
- **26 undocumented color values** (`design-system-color`): Drift from `DESIGN.md` using literals like `#111827`, `#0f172a`, `#cbd5e1`, and `#94a3b8`.
- **5 undocumented radii** (`design-system-radius`): Use of `6px`, `10px`, and `2rem` outside the design tokens.
- **5 banned side-stripe accents** (`side-tab`): Banned `border-l-4` or `border-r-4` accent lines on cards and tabs.
- **5 elastic/bouncy cubic-bezier ease curves** (`bounce-easing`): Over-animated elastic transitions (e.g. `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)`).
- **9 layout geometry animations** (`layout-transition`): Jank-inducing transitions on layout-affecting attributes (`height`, `padding`, `max-height`, `margin-top`).

**Visual overlays**:

#### Overall Impression
The interface is a highly functional clinical manager, but its visual refinement is held back by minor aesthetic inconsistencies (especially gray text on light colored backgrounds), layout shifts during animations, and strict token drift. Eliminating the 175 gray-on-color violations is the single biggest opportunity to elevate visual precision and legibility.

---

#### What's Working
1. **Clean Navigation Layout:** The responsive sidebar and auto-hiding header (which was recently fixed) correctly adapt screen real estate for clinical workflows.
2. **Solid Clinic Domain Match:** Terminology, patient badges, and queue numbers match the actual language and workflow of medical clinics.

#### Priority Issues

##### [P1] Banned Side-Stripe Accent Borders on Cards/Tabs (`side-tab`)
- **Why it matters**: The `border-l-4` and `border-r-4` indicators look dated and clash with the 24px rounded corners of clinic cards.
- **Fix**: Remove side-stripe borders. Use a full thin border, a subtle background tint, or a distinct, detached vertical status bar indicator.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable layout`

##### [P1] Gray Text on Tinted Backgrounds (`gray-on-color`)
- **Why it matters**: 175 instances of gray text (like `text-slate-600` or `text-slate-400`) on tinted backgrounds (like `bg-sky-50`) wash out text contrast, causing high eye strain for medical staff reading records.
- **Fix**: Replace gray text on colored backgrounds with high-contrast colored text (e.g., use a darker sky/blue shade on sky background, or a white text on sky-500).
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable colorize`

##### [P2] Bouncy Easing and Elastic Motion (`bounce-easing`)
- **Why it matters**: Bouncy, elastic animations feel gimmicky and unprofessional in a clinical admin console.
- **Fix**: Replace bouncy curves (e.g. `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)`) with smooth exponential ease-out curves (e.g., `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`).
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable animate`

##### [P2] Animating Layout Geometry (`layout-transition`)
- **Why it matters**: Transitioning layout attributes (e.g., `height`, `padding`, `max-height`) forces page reflows and stutter on low-performance clinic tablets.
- **Fix**: Optimize transitions to animate hardware-accelerated properties (`opacity` and `transform` / `scale` / `translate`) instead of geometry.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable animate`

##### [P2] Design System Drift in Colors and Radii (`design-system-color` / `design-system-radius`)
- **Why it matters**: Using undocumented colors and radii undermines the cohesion of the ClinicHub design system.
- **Fix**: Map literal colors (e.g., `#111827`, `#0f172a`) and radii (e.g., `6px`, `10px`) to tokens defined in `DESIGN.md`.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable polish`

#### Persona Red Flags

**Alex (Impatient Power User)**: 
No keyboard shortcuts are available for checking in patients, moving queues, or confirming POS transactions. Alex has to navigate and click through multiple dropdowns, which slows down clinic operations during peak hours. High frustration risk.

**Sam (Accessibility-Dependent User)**:
Missing explicit keyboard focus rings on several custom selectors, and 175 gray-on-color elements fail the WCAG AA 4.5:1 contrast ratio, making text unreadable for low-vision users.

**Nurse Ploy (Clinic Nurse Coordinator - Project-Specific)**:
Multi-tasking on an iPad while checking in patients. The auto-scrolling mobile header layout shifts slightly during tab transitions (due to geometry animation transitions), causing touch targets to shift and leading to misclicks.

**Dr. Kitti (Consultant / Doctor - Project-Specific)**:
Dr. Kitti reads EMR records rapidly while consulting. The gray-on-color text contrast issue makes it difficult to read vitals quickly, forcing dr. Kitti to squint to verify HN numbers and medical descriptions.
